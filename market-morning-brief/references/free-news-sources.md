# Free News and Market Sources

Use free and public sources first. Treat pre-market news as evidence with different confidence levels.

## Source Priority

1. **Official and primary sources**
   - China: State Council, NDRC, MOF, PBOC, CSRC, exchanges, ministry websites.
   - Company: CNINFO, SSE/SZSE/BSE announcements, company investor relations.
   - Overseas: Fed, BLS, BEA, Treasury, SEC, official government sites.

2. **Market data sources**
   - AkShare for indexes, futures-like indicators where available, commodities, macro calendars, and public datasets.
   - Eastmoney public pages for A-share, sector, concept, ETF, and market dashboard clues.
   - Sina/Tencent/NetEase quote endpoints as lightweight fallback.
   - Exchange/index providers for official index and market statistics when needed.

3. **News aggregators and media**
   - Eastmoney, Sina Finance, Tencent Finance, Yicai, 21jingji, Securities Times, Shanghai Securities News.
   - Use media as a pointer unless the article itself is the primary source.

4. **Global market context**
   - Public finance pages for US indexes, ETFs, ADRs, yields, FX, commodities.
   - Prefer official macro releases for CPI, jobs, PMI, and central bank decisions.

## Evidence Quality Labels

Use these labels in the brief:

| Label | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| 官方/一手 | Official announcement, filing, regulator/ministry/exchange/company source. |
| 数据 | Market price, rate, FX, commodity, or macro data. |
| 权威媒体 | Reputable media report with attributable details. |
| 市场传闻 | Rumor, unconfirmed social/news flow, or vague source. |
| 价格反应 | Inferred from overnight asset moves rather than explicit news. |

## Reliability Rules

- Cross-check official policy and company announcements when they drive the conclusion.
- Do not quote exact market prices if the source is stale.
- Do not infer broad A-share impact from a single overseas stock unless it is sector-leading.
- Mark global-market moves as context, not destiny.
- If news is important but unverified, include it under risk/uncertainty rather than as a main driver.

## Data Map

| Need | Preferred Free Sources | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| US/global index moves | Public finance pages, AkShare where available | Use direction and magnitude; exact futures quotes may be delayed. |
| USD/CNH, yields, commodities | Public finance pages, central bank/market data, AkShare | State if data is delayed. |
| Domestic policy | Official ministry/regulator sites, Xinhua if official source is linked | Prefer primary text. |
| Company announcements | CNINFO, SSE, SZSE, BSE | Use original announcement before media interpretation. |
| Sector catalysts | Official sources + reputable media + price reaction | Distinguish actual policy from thematic hype. |
| A-share watchlist | Eastmoney/AkShare sector and concept pages | Vendor taxonomy is not official. |
